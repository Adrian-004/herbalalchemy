Respiratory Issues

Respiratory issues affect the lungs and airways, causing difficulty in breathing. Diseases like asthma, bronchitis, and pneumonia are common respiratory problems.

Diseases Related to Respiratory Issues

Asthma

Asthma is a chronic condition where the airways become inflamed, making breathing difficult.

Herbal Cures for Asthma:

  • Lagundi (Vitex negundo) - Known for its ability to relax the airways and reduce inflammation.
  • Oregano (Origanum vulgare) - Has antimicrobial properties that help with respiratory problems.
  • Tawa-Tawa (Euphorbia hirta) - Reduces mucus production and acts as an expectorant.

Bronchitis

Bronchitis is the inflammation of the bronchial tubes, leading to coughing, wheezing, and difficulty breathing.

Herbal Cures for Bronchitis:

  • Lagundi (Vitex negundo) - Used to relieve bronchitis symptoms and reduce airway inflammation.
  • Oregano (Origanum vulgare) - Helps reduce inflammation and treat the cough associated with bronchitis.

Pneumonia

Pneumonia is an infection of the lungs that causes inflammation and fluid accumulation in the air sacs.

Herbal Cures for Pneumonia:

  • Oregano (Origanum vulgare) - Known for its antibacterial properties, helping in lung infections.
  • Lagundi (Vitex negundo) - Eases cough and breathing difficulties in pneumonia patients.

Tuberculosis (TB)

A bacterial infection that primarily affects the lungs, causing coughing, fever, and weight loss.

Herbal Cures for Tuberculosis (TB):

  • Eucalyptus (Eucalyptus globulus) -Helps clear airways and relieve congestion with expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties.
  • Licorice Root (Glycyrrhiza glabra) -Soothes the respiratory tract, reduces coughing, and has anti-inflammatory effects.
  • Ginseng (Panax ginseng) -Boosts lung health, improves energy levels, and supports respiratory function.
  • Oregano (Origanum vulgare) -Has antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties to relieve coughing and wheezing.
  • Thyme (Thymus vulgaris) -Helps clear mucus from the lungs with its expectorant properties.

Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)

A progressive lung disease that makes it hard to breathe, commonly caused by smoking.

Herbal Cures for Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD):

  • Oregano (Origanum vulgare) - Known for its antibacterial properties, helping in lung infections.
  • Lagundi (Vitex negundo) - Eases cough and breathing difficulties in pneumonia patients.
